Dhootha Web series

Trailer


"Dhootha" is a Telugu-language supernatural thriller series centered around Sagar, a journalist who discovers old newspaper clippings predicting the deaths of people around him, which then tragically come to pass. The series explores themes of journalism, crime, and the supernatural, as Sagar investigates these ominous prophecies and the mysterious events surrounding them.

Episode1



Sagar, editor-in-chief of the Samacharam newspaper, finds a crossword puzzle at a roadside restaurant. The puzzle predicts a terrible accident is about to happens

Episode2



"Editorial Cartoon," journalist Sagar Verma's life takes a darker turn when he receives another newspaper clipping with a chilling prediction, this time an editorial cartoon. Meanwhile, police detective Kranthi Shenoy is investigating the apparent suicide of a journalist and suspects foul play. Sagar, under pressure from the unfolding events and the ominous predictions, instructs Ajay Ghosh to interrogate Koti, leading to further complications and revealing a connection to Sagar's farmhouse.

Episode3



Kranthi and Ranga find an unexpected body while investigating. Sagar follows Kiran, head of research and host of "Ghost Shadows."Sagar gets another newspaper clipping with a bad prediction. As the investigation into the mysterious deaths continues, Kranti and Ranga dig up a spot where they find an unexpected dead body.

Episode4



As Kranthi's investigative net tightens around Sagar, he decides to visit Dhootha Publications in an attempt to unravel the mystery behind Sathyamurthy's book. The story delves into a shocking past. With this information Sagar goes to the light house, closing in on the truth.

Episode5




Crime Report. Sagar and Ajay find clues in old newspapers that advance their investigation. Kranthi and Ranga connect the case to Sagar's farmhouse. Clues lead to increasing tensions.
